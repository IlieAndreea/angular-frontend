Four Seasons Resort Bali Welcomes You

Accommodation

Become one with Bali’s magical energy and gentle nature. Sheltered in a calm bay along three miles of sand, our breezy villas channel an authentic Balinese village.

Experience indoor-outdoor living at its best as you dine outside in your bale (a Balinese-style pergola) by the infinity pool and lounge on the terrace. Inspired by traditional Balinese architecture, Villas are housed in a private stone-walled courtyard featuring separate thatched-roof pavilions. Each villa offers spacious indoor and outdoor living areas, lush gardens, a private pool and their own sun terrace.

Ideal for families and groups of friends, they offer privacy and seclusion in a breathtaking setting. Together with unforgettable views and authentic arts offerings, we bring you a deeper understanding of the island’s stunning landscapes and inimitable culture.

Featured Offer

Combine two exotic experiences on Bali. Try ocean living at Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay, and escape to a private sanctuary on the Ayung River at Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan. This offer includes a stay at each resort, daily breakfast, and more. For a unique check-in experience in Sayan, choose to arrive by river raft from Jimbaran Bay.
